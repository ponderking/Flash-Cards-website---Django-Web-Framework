About Flash Cards

Flash Cards is a tool designed to help increase your study efficiency.

Our aim is to create a study aid that will help facilitate for efficient memorization techniques through repeated exposure to subject related terms, and their meanings.

Flash Cards

Each flash card is created to represent a single keyword, concept, formula, or question. The front will usually present the keyword, while the back will present its definition.

Stacks

Stacks are created to divide your flash cards by subject. This helps you organize your subjects so you can easily focus on the subject you need to study.
